Made by a Mexican Family. Cooked for Oxford.

Our family recipes were born in a tiny kitchen, cooked slow by our mom, seasoned by our dad, and taste-tested by us kids. Today, we bring those same flavors to Oxford: juicy meats, homemade spice blends, and tortillas pressed fresh every day.

— El Potrero Family

Family-Tested Recipes You Can Only Get Here

Exclusive dishes – Our Quesabirria, Chorizo For You, and Azteca Especial are recipes Mom and Dad perfected over decades—no one else in Oxford makes them.

Made fresh every morning – Produce arrives daily, tortillas hit the press when you order, and every salsa and side is batch-cooked before service.

Dining with family – The Silva family runs the line, greets every guest, and fixes portions large enough to share—because "Mi casa es tu casa."
