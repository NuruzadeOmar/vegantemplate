Bringing Chiapas Kitchen Traditions to Oxford

What started as a small weekend cook-up in Alfonso’s childhood home in Chiapas is now the heart of Oxford’s newest Mexican gathering spot. After years running Quesadilla Express, the Garcias dreamed of a place where family recipes—hand-pressed tortillas, four-hour-braised beef, and fresh-grind spice blends—could shine in a warm, casual setting.

In 2024 they reopened as El Potrero, combining ranch-style hospitality with the same “Mi casa es tu casa” welcome Alfonso offers his own kids.

Mi Casa Es Tu Casa

Every tortilla, slow-braise and fresh salsa at El Potrero is made as if we’re feeding Alfonso’s own family, kids. Because if it’s not good enough for them, it won’t leave our kitchen.
